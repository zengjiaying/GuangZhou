Top Attractions in Guangzhou

Canton Tower 广州塔

Standing at 604 meters, Canton Tower is the tallest structure in Guangzhou. Visitors can enjoy panoramic views of the city from the observation deck, experience skywalking, and even dine in the rotating restaurant.

广州塔高604米，是广州最高建筑。游客可以登观景台俯瞰城市全景，体验空中漫步，甚至在旋转餐厅用餐。

Shamian Island 沙面岛

This historic island features European colonial architecture, tree-lined streets, and a peaceful atmosphere, offering a glimpse into Guangzhou's international history.

中文：沙面岛保留了欧式殖民建筑，街道林荫环绕，环境宁静，展现了广州的国际历史风貌。
